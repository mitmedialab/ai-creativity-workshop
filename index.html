Week 3
March 16th 		What are GANs?
 Students will understand:
  1. What is classification?
  2. What is generation?
  3. What is the difference between AI that classifies vs. generates?
  4. How can AI be adversarial?
  1. Unplugged colors activity - generate new colors vs. classify colors
  2. What are GANs + how are they different from other AI
  3. Human cognition vs. AI - how we generate things vs. how AI generates things
  4. GAN or not activity

March 23rd 		How do GANs work?
 Students will understand:

  3. What can a GAN do?
  4. Explore ethical implications of GANs
  1. Unplugged generator vs. discriminator activity
  2. More exampels of GANs, reinforce the ones they've already seen
  3. Unanticipated consequences of GANs
